    On ▇▇ ▇, 20▇▇, there was an noted to be an atmospheric shift in ▇ ▇▇, CA indicating a Category 3 Hurricane on the Saffir-Simpson Scale. Residents were advised to brace for the storm. At ▇:▇, when the storm was predicted to hit, a blue fog instead blanketed ▇ ▇▇ in what would later be known as the Cataclysm.

    Beings from the Otherside (called Outsiders from this point onwards) emerged from the fog, possessing destructive tendencies as they were introduced into the city of ▇ ▇▇. The >Outsiders possessed remarkable physical abilities and a lack of ability to communicate with the human race. Early attempts to fight against the Outsiders failed, until an international organization called the Garden utilized its operators to win the first front in 20▇▇.

    A hundred years after the Cataclysm in present day, much of the world still remains in a struggle with attempting to manage the Outsiders breaching globally. The Garden, an international organization of exorcists, has recently opened their newest branch, Blue Lilac, in the former city of Los Angeles (now renamed as The Edge due to the density of the blue fog alongside the coastal waters). The Edge is an experimental city to see if humans can return to living in the territory, focusing on survival, redevelopment and rehabilitation.

    New Blue Lilac operatives could think of themselves as unlucky. Blue Lilac is known among all its sister branches for being hastily built together, understaffed, overworked, and tasked with defending the most dangerous city in all of North America. Other operatives might think of themselves as lucky. while still others would consider those very people to be insane, though it may very well be the self-same attitude that brought them to Blue Lilac in the first place.

    With some joining to fulfill their idealistic aspirations, and others conscripted as parts of deals to keep their abilities in check, many would see the city as a grave, while others would see it as a gateway to freedom.

    the state of the world

    The year is currently 123 AC (After the Cataclysm). The current state of human infrastructure has returned to 80% of the functionality of the time before the Cataclysm. Though a majority of the cities from the past before the Cataclysm have remained the same, others have changed drastically after more than a hundred years of upheaval and strife.

    Not only has the blue fog remained, but Otherside activity runs rampant in hotspots around the globe where its reach has extended considerably. Many locations overtaken by blue fog are often inhabited by Outsiders, with some locations more regulated than others. Most of the safer location are typically those with large branches of The Garden, an international organization who regulate Otherside activity. Other locations without the presence of The Garden leaves them susceptible to crime and a high rate of mortality due to the general chaos the Outsiders bring.

    Formerly, researchers believed that the blue fog was directly tied to population density. Rural locations rarely had appearances of blue fog, while urban locations had considerably larger amounts of blue fog activity. However, this theory has been debunked with the increase of blue fog in both rural and urban locations equally, with seemingly no rhyme nor reason as to why these hotspots appear.

    While still a mystery to many, The Garden aims to not only bring stability to the world, but investigate the source of the blue fog, and understand what started the Cataclysm in the first place.

    the edge

    Formerly known as the city of Los Angeles, The Edge of the Great Divide (though known to most as simply The Edge), is a city undergoing rapid rehabilitation and rejuvenation efforts. The city itself had been abandoned during the early days of the Cataclysm, as blue fog sweeping in from the ocean destroyed most of the city. However, as the global reclamation efforts from The Garden progressed and cohabitation with Outsiders stabilized, many had started to come back to The Edge, making efforts to restore the city to livable conditions.

    The Edge has made major progress compared to its early days. Despite the inherent danger of its coast straddling the blue fog and the constant influx of Outsiders, The Edge continues to receive a wide influx of immigrants who aim to settle down in the city.

    If there’s one silver lining to the chaos, it’s that rent prices have managed to fall down to affordable levels.

    Due to the Cataclysm, both the geography and weather have shifted drastically, rendering it unrecognizable. Few remnants of its pre-Cataclysm life remains, some including the dilapidated Hollywood sign among the hills, the broken skyline of downtown, and remnants of the famed Sunset Boulevard. Though there are still sunny dry summers, The Edge receives cool autumns and even colder winters. Snow is no longer limited to the peaks of the mountains but also falls regularly during the winter, with the local fauna remaining all year round.

    Members of the City Board have attempted to develop The Edge into a tourist destination. With sunny summers, snowy winters, and the salty ocean breeze all packed into one area, The Edge would make for a perfect vacation spot… if you ignore all the dangers to your life on the daily.

    about
  • BL;UE is a casual rp group with a focus on art and writing. You will require an illustrated reference to apply for this group, but art ability is not necessary to participate in BL;UE.
  • Activity is based primarily off member-run events and episodic mod-run events with plot. Content ranges from light-hearted slice of life to action, horror, and violence. Please take this into consideration before applying.
  • This group is rated 18+ due to the nature of potentially sensitive and dangerous situations. All members must be 21+ by the time this group opens. Please be courteous to your fellow group members and be mindful of their comfort when posting in the server, especially when dealing with potentially heavy themes.
  • There is no explicit NSFW allowed in this server.
  • A Discord account is required to apply for this group. A DeviantArt account is preferred for app storage in the case Discord is down.
  • Standard group rules apply. Be respectful, no god-modding, metagaming, disrespectful shipchasing. Things that happen in-character are not the same as out-of-character. Hate speech, discriminatory content, plagiarism, and theft is not tolerated.
  • Please keep disagreements and drama outside of the group and in dms. I will be acting on a reactionary nature in regards to conflict; please bring things that need attention to me directly or through an intermediary. In many cases, it’s possible I won’t realize what is going on until I am explicitly told.
  • I will be reserving the right to take any action I feel is necessary for the wellbeing of those in the group, such as removing one or both parties from the group.
